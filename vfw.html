Web + Contents Developer at Vancouver Fashion Week's Micro Macro Magazine

Jan. - Apr. 2021

Micro Macro Magazine is a digital magazine company under Vancouver Fashion Week. I was a web + content developer and graphic design intern at VFW, completing various tasks from growing magazine press Micro Macro Magazine. I was involved in the Micro-Macro Magazine team to develop their new pleasant website and contents for their Instagram and magazine to engage audiences. Conducting researches on current online magazine trends and incredible designers in various fields, our team launched #designmonday, #innovationwednesday and featuring artists. I was enrolled in researching, conducting and graphic designing while developing the contents and kept working uploading posts. Moreover, I worked for Micro Macro Magazines' commercial video to promote the brand in the upcoming VFW SS21.

Click Here to Explore Mirco Macro Magazine

#DesignMonday & #InnovationWednesday

Both #DesignMonday and #InnovationWednesday introduce influential designers and design works for our modern society. They contain diverse stories sharing designers from various areas such as industrial, architecture and even automobile.

Every week, I shared fabulous design works from different designers by researching the most influential designers in the 21 century. In addition to creating a post, I designed several graphic designs for #DesignMonday and #InnovationWednesday to advertise them efficiently by grabbing audiences' attention.
